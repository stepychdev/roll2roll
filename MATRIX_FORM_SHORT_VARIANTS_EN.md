## Short Description (≈450-550 chars)
Jackpot SocialFi Roulette is a fair, open, on-chain jackpot game on Solana. Players can enter with USDC or other tokens (via Jupiter routing/swaps), compete in a shared pot, and win through VRF-based settlement. The product combines SocialFi loops (live pot, odds, wheel, chat, history) with degen UX, mobile-first interaction design, and a production-oriented security/ops posture (multisig governance, tests, smoke checks).

## PSG1 Integration (short)
We use Solana as the game state and settlement layer: rounds, deposits, settlement, claims, and refunds are on-chain. Winner selection uses VRF for provable fairness. Wallet-native interactions and Jupiter-powered routing make DeFi actions part of the core gameplay loop, not just a side feature.
